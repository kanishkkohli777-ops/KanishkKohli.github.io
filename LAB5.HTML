RECIPE PROCEDURE TO MAKE TEA

  1. Take one cup of water in glass.
  2. Add cup of water in a steel bowl.
  3. let water boil for atleast 2 minutes .
  4. Then add tea leaves and ginger if you need.
  5. Add 2 teaspoon sugar (optional).
  6. Add 1 and a half cup of milk in it.
  7. Then wait for 10-15 minutes and tea will be ready.
